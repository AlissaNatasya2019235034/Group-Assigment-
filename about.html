BACKGROUND

The LipViu business was established on 1st September 2021 by 5 partners, which is Syawal Ikhwan as the founder together with Izham Hamizi, Nurul Hasanah, Alisa Natasya and Nureen Jazlina as business partners. The establishment of this business is based on the inspiration of Syawal Ikhwan and also Nurul Hasanah. The initial capital of this business is sourced from the partner's savings of RM 90,000 in a ratio of 2: 1: 1: 1: 1. 3 out of 5 partners for the business have become models for LipViu products namely Nureen Jazlina, Nurul Hasanah and also Alisa Natasya.
